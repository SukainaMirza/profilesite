We're Free, Are We?

Yes, a milestone has been achieved
We, the human race, are free
free to live, love and die,
die, within your boundary.


certainly, we are free to dress,
dress the way you seem fit
though watch out young woman,
your character just maybe judged on the size of that slit


Yes, the human race is free
and why wouldnt it be?
human basic rights have been imposed,
yet the other day,a maiden was used and disposed


With all respect young man, we are given more freedom than our past race
but whats the point of this freedom,
for shackles tighten when we quicken our pace?
better yet, these shackles are not physical,
they come in forms of power, family or customs.
where consequences stop us to look in the eye of the oppressor
for what he may bring upon this system


if this my friend is what freedom is,
then yet again i ask
I'd want to give captivity a try,
for what we may have misjudged meanings
in our attempt to reach the sky
